# Lighting presets for the `edit` pipeline

Append one of these strings to the Gemini prompt when the user described a
mood but no specific light. Use English regardless of user's language.

## Window light (default for indoor plants/pots)

`soft natural window light from camera-left, warm 3500K, gentle highlight on
the subject's right side with soft falloff, subtle window-frame shadow on
floor, photorealistic`

## Golden hour

`golden hour sunlight streaming from upper-left at low angle, warm 3000K,
long soft shadows, dust particles in beams, slight lens haze, photorealistic`

## Studio softbox (clean product look)

`studio softbox key light from camera-left at 45 degrees, large diffuser,
fill card on right, seamless background, no harsh shadows, even color
temperature 5000K, commercial product photography`

## Overcast / diffuse (botanical catalog)

`overcast diffuse daylight, no visible sun, even shadows, neutral 5500K,
no color cast, every leaf clearly visible, botanical reference photo`

## Evening / cozy

`warm tungsten lamp from upper-right, 2700K, ambient room light, gentle
bokeh in background, intimate evening mood, photorealistic`

## Dramatic side rim

`dramatic rim light from camera-right, dark moody background, low key
exposure, single highlight defining the silhouette, fine art still life`

## Bright sunny window

`bright direct sunbeam through window from camera-right at midday, sharp
hard shadows, high contrast, vibrant greens, photorealistic`

## Studio black

`subject on a polished black surface with subtle reflection, single soft
key light from above, deep shadow falloff, dark gradient background,
luxury product photography`

## Studio white

`subject on a seamless white cyclorama, two softbox lights eliminating
shadows, clean ecommerce product shot, no color cast`
