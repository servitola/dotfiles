# Style briefs — 10 fixed variants

Each variant agent gets exactly one brief from this list. The brief is
verbatim text — don't paraphrase, don't merge briefs, don't invent extras.

---

## 1. Дизайн-борд с легендой
Big scene photo on the left filling the full collage height, flush with
the left edge. A second scene photo (if present) sits at the top-right
about the same width as the right strip, anchored to the top-right
corner. Subjects placed across the canvas: one large foreground subject
slightly left of centre, smaller subjects scattered (top-left, bottom
right, on the windowsill of any visible window). Add a 180 px legend
strip at the bottom of the canvas with the bg colour, list every
subject with a numbered marker (cream circle, dark stroke, dark
numeral). Two-column legend, Arial 24pt.

## 2. Журнальный разворот
Two large scene photos side by side, equal widths, full canvas height
(no bottom strip). Subjects float over the seam between them, mostly
cut off at the top or bottom edge to feel editorial. No markers, no
captions. Add a single thin white horizontal hairline 30 px from the
bottom across the full width for a print-magazine feel.

## 3. Сетка-мозаика
Strict 3×2 photo grid filling the full canvas with even gutters (12 px
gap, bg colour). Cells alternate scene tiles and subject tiles; subject
tiles get a soft circular vignette so the subject sits centred on a bg
square. No labels.

## 4. Скрапбук с лёгким наклоном
Asymmetric scrapbook layout: scene photos rotated by small angles
(±3°...±8°), with thin off-white paper borders (8 px) and soft drop
shadows. Subjects pasted on top at varied scales, also slightly
rotated. Add hand-lettered-style numbered tags (slight rotation,
Georgia Bold) next to each subject. Background colour darkened by
~15 % for depth.

## 5. Минимализм с большим воздухом
Single small scene photo (≤ 45 % of canvas height) anchored bottom-left.
Subjects placed sparsely with lots of breathing room. Use the lightest
plausible bg (lighten the input bg by 30 %). Caption-style sans-serif
labels under each subject (tiny, 14 pt, very light grey). Reserve the
upper-right two-thirds of the canvas as empty bg.

## 6. Тёмная атмосфера
Deep moody bg (darken input bg by ~50 % toward near-black). Scene
photos rendered at 80 % brightness with a subtle warm vignette.
Subjects lit from one side (left): brighten left half of each
subject by ~15 %, darken right half by ~20 %. Single large subject
in foreground, others scattered behind, smaller. No markers.

## 7. Светлый эфир
Cream/eggshell bg ((238, 232, 218)). Scene photos with rounded corners
(radius 24 px). Subjects scaled medium, arranged in a loose circle
around an empty centre. Add 60 px white margin around the whole canvas
edge. Dotted divider lines between regions. No bottom legend strip;
subject names written next to each subject in serif italic.

## 8. Современный редакторский
Two scene photos: large one on the right (about 60 % of canvas width),
small one tucked top-left. Bold sans-serif numerals (Arial Bold 96 pt)
in the bg colour darkened by 50 %, placed BEHIND each subject so the
numeral peeks out from one side. Legend at the bottom as a single
horizontal line of comma-separated short labels in 18 pt regular.

## 9. Ботанический атлас
Off-white bg ((245, 240, 230)). Subjects arranged in a 2-row gallery
across the centre of the canvas, all at the same baseline (their pots
align), evenly spaced. Scene photos rendered as small thumbnail
postage-stamps in the top corners. Add a thin double-line frame
around the whole canvas, 30 px from the edge. Hand-drawn-looking
serif italic numbers (1-N) above each subject, no marker circles.

## 10. Мудборд-наслоение
Free-form overlap: every photo and subject overlaps its neighbours.
Scene photos rotated ±2-5°, varied sizes. Subjects layered in front,
some bleeding off the canvas edges. Drop shadows on every element.
No legend, no markers. Pure visual composition. Background is the
input bg colour with a subtle vertical gradient (top 10 % darker,
bottom 10 % lighter).
